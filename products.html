BrE - Elevator smart condition monitoring

Elevators is huge industry that is distributed omni places around us. from residence complex to business malls and industrial factories, hospitals you name it. maintenance of such a big distributed industry is off the most important. Emergence knowledge of artificial intelligence transform status que and provide new era to monitor and predict condition of system. prediction of system condition help to reduction maintenance cost and improve performance, safety and reliability. We provide Bright elevator as a solution for above purpose that capable of monitoring elevator condition and predict probability of failure in future. How Bright Elevator (BrE) works: BrE install on elevator and gather information from sensors and send to cloud. cloud is intelligent system that analyses data and provide report of elevator condition.
